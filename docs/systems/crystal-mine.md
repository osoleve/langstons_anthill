# Crystal Mine

*"Crystal Cave"*

Strange formations glitter in the dark.

## Stats

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Type** | Generator |
| **Generates** | Crystals: 0.001/tick |
| **Consumes** | Nothing |
| **Location** | (2, 0) |

## Notes

Crystals are slow to accumulate. They are not food, not building material. They are something else entirely.

Crystals power the Resonance Chamber and may have other uses.
