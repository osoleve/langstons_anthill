# Resources

Resources are what the colony produces, consumes, and accumulates.

## Primary Resources

| Resource | Source | Use |
|----------|--------|-----|
| **Dirt** | Dig Site | Raw material |
| **Nutrients** | Compost Heap, Corpses, Resonance | Food production |
| **Fungus** | Fungus Farm | Ant food |
| **Crystals** | Crystal Mine | Resonance Chamber, crafting |
| **Ore** | Ore Mine | Jewelry crafting |

## The Death Economy

When ants die, their corpses are processed by undertakers back into nutrients. This creates a cycle:

1. Nutrients grow fungus
2. Fungus feeds ants
3. Ants eventually die
4. Corpses become nutrients

The Crystal Resonance Chamber was built to provide nutrients without death.
